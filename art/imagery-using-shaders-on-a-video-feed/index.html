The Project

Timelines: January 2022

Project: Using the Webcam feed and p5js to create interesting imagery

Team: Solo

Tools: p5.js, GLSL Shaders, MacBook Pro Web Camera

Old Film Reel

I created a old film reel effect. It had a raw and rugged look.

The image moved vertically at an uneven speed like an old film reel.

Stretched Bottom

I stretched out the bottom with an “atan” function. It was interesting to hold vertical items near the edge where the stretch started..

It reminded me of illustrations where body part proportions are exaggerated.
