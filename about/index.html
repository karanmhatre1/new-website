About me

I'm a designer, coder, and painter from India. Through my career I have worn many hats - a front-end developer, a product manager, and now a product designer. My versatility in skill-sets gives me a great perspective when it comes to product thinking and problem solving.

In December 2021, I moved to Berlin.

I did my graduation in Computer Engineering, and over time moved to crafting digital user experiences.

I also create generative art using p5JS, and paint postcards from places that I travel to.

Career Summary

  • (2015-2017) Product Manager at Practo Here I built a product to help patients to find the right hospital for their treatments (Read more). I also helped scale up the Practo Tab product - a tablet device that helps doctors register patients and manage billing.
  • (2017-2020) Senior Product Manager at GOQii Here I designed and launched GOQii Play - A platform for live online health and fitness classes and scaled it up to over 50K daily active users.
  • (2020-2022) Lead UX Designer at Byju Future School from 2020-2022. Here I designed a digital in-class and post class experience for 6-14 year old students to learn how to code.
  • (2022-Present) Lead Product Designer at Lilt Inc since April 2021. I work closely with translators and craft A.I. driven product features to help make them more efficient.

Other interesting things

  • (2015) taught Maths and English to 6th Grade students with the Akaksha Foundation in Mumbai.
  • (2017) exhibited my artwork (zines) with Bombay Underground.
  • (2019) exhibited my artwork (zines) with Gaysi Family .
  • (2018) I made an editoral illustration for Verve Magazine
  • (2020) Mentored design students on MentorCruise.
  • (2021) Taught UX design to students to help them build better apps at Byjus Future School (Teaching UX Design to Kids).
