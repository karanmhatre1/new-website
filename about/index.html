About me

I'm a designer, coder, and painter from India. Through my career I have worn many hats - a front-end developer, a product manager, and now a product designer. My versatility in skill-sets gives me a great perspective when it comes to product thinking and problem solving.

In December 2021, I moved to Berlin with my wife.

I did my graduation in Computer Engineering, and over time moved to crafting User Experience Design.

I also create generative art using p5JS, and paint postcards from places that I travel to.

Career Summary

  • I was a Product Manager at Practo from 2015-2017. Here I built a product to help patients to find the right hospital for their treatments (Read more). I also helped scale up the Practo Tab product - a tablet device that helps doctors register patients and manage billing.
  • I was a Senior Product Manager at GOQii from 2017-2020. Here I designed and launched GOQii Play - A platform for live online health and fitness classes and scaled it up to over 50K daily active users.
  • I was a Lead UX Designer at Byju Future School from 2020-2022. Here I designed a digital in-class and post class experience for 6-14 year old students to learn how to code.
